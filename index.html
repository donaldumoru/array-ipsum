arrayIpsum is a simple, copy-and-paste generator for arrays of dummy data: 'words', numbers, 'emails', 'usernames', and {small objects}. It’s perfect for learning and quick prototypes.

Usage

Pick a category, choose how many items you need, click Generate, then Copy the result and Paste into your code.

Credits & Disclaimer

arrayIpsum does not create or own the content it generates. Names and user data are provided by the RandomUser API, and random words are provided by the Random Word API. All generated data is intended for testing, learning, and prototyping only.
